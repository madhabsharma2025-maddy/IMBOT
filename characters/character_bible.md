# Character Bible

## The Family
- **Mom** — fast, sharp, intelligent. Solves problems quickly (sometimes too quickly — running joke).
- **Dad** — slow, deeply honest. Never lies even when it'd be easier; gets things done the long, right way.
- **Son** — agreeable, listens to everyone. Growth arc: learning when to voice his own opinion.
- **Daughter** — clever, resourceful. Often spots the real problem before anyone else.

## The Animal Friends
- **Fox** — the moral center. Every episode ends with Fox delivering the lesson/motto, warm and simple.
- **Rabbit** — proud, sarcastic, "doesn't care." Learns the lesson every episode, resets to attitude by the next (the comedy is in the relapse).
- **Panda** — easygoing goofball. Physical comedy, fart jokes, chaos-lightener during tense moments.
- **Snake** — elegant, graceful, unfailingly positive. Helper/peacemaker, reframes problems kindly.
- **Monkey** — small, messy, mischievous. Makes a mess wherever he goes, instantly forgiven for being tiny and lovable.

## Show Rules
- Clean content only — no bad language, everything admirable and kid-safe.
- Every episode ends with Fox stating a life lesson in one clear line.
- Rabbit always relapses into his ego/attitude by the closing tag scene — this is the running joke, not a flaw to fix permanently.
- Kids should laugh often — Panda and Monkey carry most physical comedy.
- 30-minute runtime target per episode.
